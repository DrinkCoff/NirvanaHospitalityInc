Working At Nirvana Hospitality

Without a dedicated team who enjoy their job, a company can't survive. We strive to make sure our associates want to come to work each day and describe it as the reason to be at their best each day. The atmosphere Nirvana Hospitality provides for individuals while at work is one of inspiration, opportunity, and success. Since 2005, we've been on a growth spurt and are achieving great success at development and management of the most renowned brands in the hotel industry. Associates working for Nirvana Hospitality must be accountable for their actions and driven by their goals. With much ongoing training and education provided, it's an opportunity to learn from experienced professionals and to grow as an individual as well as an associate of Nirvana Hospitality.

High Octane Careers

At Nirvana Hospitality, we are moving and developing at a quick pace. New challenges and experiences are keys to keeping top performers engaged. If you are a motivated and energetic individual looking to be a part of something great, if you are looking for a career with opportunity around every corner, if you hold yourself to a higher standard,Nirvana Hospitality is where you belong. We value hard work, recognize your contributions and reward the above and beyond. We expect the best, but we also give you the tools, training and mentoring to help you succeed in your career.

Benefits

At Nirvana Hospitality, we know our associates are our greatest assets. We invest in their futures, their careers, and their lives. We believe that by taking care of our associates, they will in turn take care of our guests, and in turn everything will fall in place. That is why we reward their hard work with the most comprehensive benefits.

We understand the importance of quality benefits for you and your family. It's essential for a happy and healthy work and home life. Here's a glimpse of what we offer:

  • Medical Plan Designs
  • Vision Care
  • Flexible Spending (medical and child care)
  • Complimentary & Discount Hotel Room Plans
  • Dental Care

Career Advancement

Working for Nirvana Hospitality is not just a career but an opportunity to grow and advance. One of the greatest assets we offer our professionals is our ability to develop and quickly advance our associates. We recognize hard work and often promote from within the company. We give you the tools to be successful in your career. What you do with it is up to you.
