New Developments

As an innovative company, our development team is aggressively seeking new development. A strong outlook for hotel performance in a liquid market, there is an imminent need to pursue development of new-build hotels where there are distinct of opportunities. We research opportunities for the future. We prefer to manage our development growth in order to have a number of projects in the pipeline at all times in diverse segments and locations.

It takes experience to successfully conceive, design, finance, build and open a hotel. This is why our team goes through careful due diligence, extensive analysis and evaluation with every project. Each market is different, and every opportunity must align with the company's vision.

Matching each brand for each marketplace is an area of expertise. We research franchise-building incentives, and explore all opportunities. We only partner with the elite franchisors and utilize their marketplace position as a barrier to entry.

Nirvana Hospitality has a particular culture of commitment to its investors. We are passionate about our responsiveness to our development objectives and goals.

Redevelopments

Nirvana Hospitality has strong level of knowledge in upgrading and realigning an asset. This expertise has been recognized by franchisors, lenders, owners, and local governments.

Since 2005, when Nirvana Hospitality acquired its first hotel asset in Indianapolis the company has had great success in the redevelopment and repositioning of numerous hotel properties. All of Nirvana Hospitality projects are meticulously planned, designed and executed.

Project Management

Our professional development project management and purchasing/procurement team provides the experience and knowledge to format and implement strategies that maximize value, increase opportunity and prevent obstacles for your project.

  • Ongoing support of strategic research and market development
  • Appropriate brand selection
  • Development of realistic pro forma expectations based on extensive due diligence of the market conditions
  • Ensure timely budget project execution
  • Strong vendor relationships that facilitate purchasing of the right materials and services at the right cost
  • Procurement services for literally every operating supply and service required for opening
  • Effective negotiations with elite brands to ensure their standards are achieved while maximizing the return on owner's investment
