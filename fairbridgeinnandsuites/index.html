Welcome To Fair Bridge Inn & Suites Marion

Swimming Pool and Breakfast are closed due to Covid-19

Located 12.9 km east of Indiana Wesleyan University, this Marion, Indiana hotel serves a daily continental breakfast to all guests. Each guest room is equipped with free WiFi.

Cable TV with HBO is included in every non-smoking room at Fair Bridge Inn & Suites Marion. Guests can enjoy the convenience of in-room tea and coffee-making facilities, as well.

An indoor pool is available at Fair Bridge Inn & Suites Marion. A fitness centre and launderette facilities are located on site.
The James Dean Gallery is 24.1 km away from this hotel. Walnut Creek Golf Club can be reached in 14 minutes’ drive.
