History

Nirvana Hospitality is one of the largest privately owned progressive companies specializing in providing investor and career opportunities, development, construction, and management services. The company has matured into developing and operating over 10 hotels.

The company continues to set itself apart with a diversified portfolio including economy, budget and mid-scale hotels

Our complete services in business development, construction, and hotel management. Our vision is to become the most respected management company in the hospitality industry.

Our mission is to provide our guests with superior customer service by attracting and retaining associates with the highest integrity and providing opportunities for professional and personal growth. Our values are centered on our associates, guests, communities, and the success of the company. We conduct our business with integrity, efficiency, and respect, providing our associates an environment to excel while enriching our local communities.

Profits and growth are essential to fulfill our responsibilities to guests, associates, and owners. We encourage growth without compromising quality. Our financial success allows us the freedom to pursue these philosophies.
